Aira's story

Aira was born from a love for delicate scents and the desire to create warmth in every home. Inspired by the timeless elegance of peony blooms, we craft candles that soothe the soul and elevate everyday moments. With every flickering flame, Aira brings a touch of serenity, making your space feel like home.

VISION

Aira envisions becoming a leading brand in handcrafted scented candles, bringing sophistication and soothing fragrance to every home.

MISSION

Aira's mission is to create exquisite scented candles that elevate everyday moments, bringing serenity, tranquility, and elegance to every space.

IDEA

Aira was born from the idea of capturing nature's most delicate scents, especially peony blooms, to transform ordinary spaces into havens of elegance and serenity.

Brand Equity

Aira infuses elegance, serenity, and nature's beauty into every scented candle. Using pure ingredients and delicate fragrances, we create moments of warmth, comfort, and sophistication. Each flame transforms your space into a sanctuary of peace and timeless charm.
